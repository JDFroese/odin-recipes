Sweet Tea

Descriptions

This sweet tea is a classic Southern drink perfect for hot summer days!

Ingredients

  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 6 tea bags
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 6 cups cool water

Steps

  1. Sprinkle a pinch of bakign soda into a 64-ounch, heat proof glass pitcher. Pour in boiling water and add tea bags. Cover and allow to steep for 15 minutes.
  2. Remove tea bags and discard; stir in sugar until dissolved. Pour in cool water; refrigerate until cold; about 3 hours.
